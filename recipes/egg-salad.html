Egg Salad Recipe

An egg salad sandwich!

Description

This egg salad with ranch dressing is simple but still delicious. Good for any occasion, have a family that likes leftovers? Well, this is the perfect recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 6 Hard-Cooked Eggs, Chopped
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Cucumber (optional)
  • 3 Tablespoons of Ranch Dressing
  • 1 Tablespoon of Mustard
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix together eggs, cucumber, ranch dressing, mustard, salt, and black pepper into a large bowl until well combined
  2. Simply serve and enjoy.

Nutritional Facts

  • Calories: 176
  • Fat: 14g
  • Carbs: 2g
  • Protein: 10g
