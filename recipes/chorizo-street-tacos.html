Chorizo Street Taco Recipe

An authentic Chorizo Street Taco!

Description

The following recipe is for Chorizo Street Tacos that you can make in your own home! Craving any of those street tacos you catch at the most random times from the local street vendor? Wait no-longer, make them for you, and your whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chorizo Sausage Link
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce
  • 4 Corn Tortillas
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chopped Onions
  • 2 Tablespoons of Chopped Fresh Cilantro (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine crumbled chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce in any size bowl.
  2. Heat skillet over medium-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp for 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Heat tortillas in reserved grease in the skillet over medium heat until warmed, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Stack 2 tortillas for each taco, then fill with chorizo, onion, and optionally cilantro.

Nutritional Facts

  • Fat: 13g
  • Carbs: 26g
